Hall Thruster Diagnostics

Perfecting the Thrusters for Psyche

Overview

When the Psyche Mission launches in 2022, it will be the first time humankind will be visiting an entirely metal asteroid. But how will we get there? The Psyche Mission is equipped with electronic Hall Thrusters which will be used as the main propulsion system once it has left our atmosphere! Since there will be no chance to test the thrusters in space, different diagnostic tests have been devised on Earth to analyze how the thrusters will perform in space. There are two important diagnostic tests for the thrusters to pass, the Faraday Probe Diagnostics and the Retards Potential Analyzer Diagnostics.
